About Our Brand

Our CamClick brand include a lens for focusing light, an image sensor to capture the image, a display screen for viewing, and various settings for controlling exposure and focus. They also have features like automatic modes, zoom capabilities, flash, and memory for storing photos.

Optical System:

  • Lens: Focuses light onto the image sensor.
  • Aperture: Controls the amount of light entering the camera.
  • Shutter: Controls the duration of light exposure.
  • Image Sensor: Converts light into electronic signals.

Image Capture & Processing:

  • Resolution: Measured in megapixels, determines the maximum image size and sharpness.
  • ISO: Sensor sensitivity to light, allowing for shooting in various lighting conditions.
  • White Balance: Adjusts colors to compensate for different lighting conditions.
  • Automatic Modes: Simplify shooting for different scenarios (e.g., portrait, landscape).
  • Focus: Adjusts the lens to ensure a sharp image.
